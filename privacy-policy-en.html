üîí What Makes MyVPN Different

MyVPN is designed with privacy-by-design principles. Unlike traditional VPN services, MyVPN puts you in complete control:

  • You control your own servers - We don't operate VPN servers
  • You manage your data - Your traffic goes through your chosen servers
  • We don't see your activity - Your connections are between you and your servers
